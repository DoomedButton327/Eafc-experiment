Fixtures & Scheduling

  • Fixtures are sent between 00:00–10:00 SAST (GMT+2), Monday to Saturday, excluding public holidays.
  • Deadline for results is 23:59 same day. NO LATE SUBMISSIONS ACCEPTED.
  • You cannot send a first message to your opponent after 19:00.
  • Fixtures display the first 4 letters of each opponent's WhatsApp username.

Result Submission

  • Submit a screenshot of your result within a 15-minute grace period after the match.
  • If contact was attempted, submit screenshot evidence within a 6-hour grace period — only after 19:00.
  • If nothing is submitted when grace period ends, the game is a draw.
  • Tag both admins in the Mettlestate group when submitting results or evidence.
  • All league messages must go on the Mettlestate group. No direct messaging.

No-Shows & Forfeits

  • If opponent doesn't arrive at agreed time — automatic forfeit after 10-minute grace.
  • If your opponent sets a time and is 30 minutes late, you are awarded the win.
  • Failure to follow this results in a forfeit or draw at admin discretion.

Postponements — 20 Per Season

  • Each player receives 20 postponements per season total. This includes games postponed by opponents against you.
  • Once you reach 0 postponements, any further postponement attempt results in an AUTO-FORFEIT — your opponent is awarded 3-0 automatically, no exceptions.
  • Use postponements wisely — there are no refunds or bonus allocations during the season.
  • Exceptions for genuine emergencies (broken phone, family bereavement, hospitalisation) are at admin discretion only — submit evidence in the group chat.
  • Postponement requests must be made via the Mettlestate group tagging admins before the fixture deadline.

Disconnections

  • Immediately report to admin and opponent. Arrange a rematch.
  • If network is the cause, provide 3–5 screenshots at the 10-minute in-game mark, or a video if possible.
  • If a goal was scored before the lag, the match replays with a handicap scoreline.
  • No evidence = straight forfeit. No excuses.

Strictly Prohibited

  • KickOff Glitch — forbidden. Caught with proof = forfeit.
  • Cross Spamming — max 5 attempts per match. Exceeded with proof = forfeit.
  • Lob Ball Spamming — max 5 attempts per match. Exceeded with proof = forfeit.
  • Passive Playing / Time-Wasting — wasting 10 minutes game-time with possession = loss. Requires 3 screenshots or short video.
  • Breaching Mettlestate community rules = removal by admins.

Disputes & Admin

  • All disputes handled by organisers, helpers, and admin. Raise it in the group.
  • Admin decisions are final.
  • Rules may be updated between seasons at organiser discretion.
  • Visit mettlestate.com for community and general rules.

Astral Friendly League — Addendum

Additional rules for the Mettlestate × Astral Friendly League:

  • You have 23 hours (00:00–23:00) to play. Unplayed games = draw.
  • Proof of contact must be submitted before 23:00 and show contact made before 20:00 to be awarded no-show win.
  • LATE RESULTS OR REPORTS ARE NOT ACCEPTED.
  • All standard conduct rules apply here too.
